Jazz/Blue Bars

Club Deluxe

Website: http://www.pizza-deluxe.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Moderate
Description: Absolutely insane pizza. Home-made dough, home-made pesto, home-made everything. The only draft beer is Lagunitas IPA and this can only be a good thing. Also, the greyhounds are fresh. Take it from a Haight street local, this place is one cool spot.
Savanna Jazz

Website: http://www.savannajazz.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Moderate
Description: What seems to set Savanna Jazz apart from all others is their depth and breadth of musical prowess displayed so consistently. At the top of it all is David Byrd, a charismatic, ebullient and truly gifted musician who I first saw in the Powell Street Bart Station and have followed ever since.

Sheba Piano Lounge

Website: http://shebapianolounge.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Moderate
Description: Live jazz, relaxed ambiance, delicious food and affordable prices

Cafe Claude

Website: http://cafeclaude.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Expensive
Description: good selection of drinks, nice settings inside and outside, friendly staff and service. Accommodating for small to medium groups.
SFJazz Center

Website: http://www.sfjazz.org/center/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Expensive
Description: it's only in its second season, and already this venue has attracted big names on its concert roster: Wynton Marsalis, Branford Marsalis, Esperanza Spaulding, Cassandra Wilson, and Rosanne Cash to name just a few.

Boom Boom Room

Website: http://www.boomboomblues.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Moderate
Description: Boom Boom Room is awesome. I'll make this short - GREAT live music, awesome crowd (mid 20s to early 30s), beautiful chicks who can really dance, decently priced drinks and the place is pretty big.
