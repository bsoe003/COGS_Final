Family Activities

Aquarium of the Bay

Website: http://www.aquariumofthebay.org/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: $19.95/person
Description: Explore the magic, beauty and wonder of San Francisco Bay at Aquarium of the Bay. Be able to see the magical life of sea creatures in their psuedo natural habitat.

Ghirdaelli Square

Website: http://www.ghirardellisq.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Free
Description: Relax and enjoy the atmosphere, or walk around and explore the famous area known for its chocolate.

Rainforest Cafe

Website: http://www.rainforestcafe.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Moderate
Description: Part adventure, part restaurant and wholly entertaining for the whole family.

Giant's Baseball Game

Website: http://sanfrancisco.giants.mlb.com/sf/ballpark/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: $67/person
Description: Come enjoy watching America's favorite pastime and support the amazingly fun baseball team , the San Francisco Giants.

Museum of Fine Arts

Website: https://www.famsf.org/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: $10/person
Description: Here you can see a big part of San Francisco culture and revel in the art that has helped make San Francisco what it is today.

Visit the Sea Lions

Website: http://www.pier39.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Free
Description: Sea Lions are one of the best attractions because you get to watch them in their natural habitat and admire how they live as a species.
