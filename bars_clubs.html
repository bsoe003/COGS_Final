Bars with Clubs

Toronado

Website:http://www.toronado.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Affordable
Description: Nicer "dive bar" with cool and hip "San Francisco" vibe and interesting craft beers.

Smuggler's Cove

Website: http://smugglerscovesf.com/trapdoor/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Moderate
Description: The drink menu includes over 70 cocktails made from premium spirits and liqueurs, housemade ingredients, and 100% fresh citrus juiced in-house daily.

Speakeasy Ales & Lagers

Website: http://www.goodbeer.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Inexpensive
Description: Speakeasy has tasty beers, and their tap room is warm and inviting. I've been there a few times over the past year and always had a great time. The bartenders are nice, and the "Medicine Cabinet" is always stocked. You can get bombers and growlers - the growlers are especially convenient for those limited edition beers on tap that you can't otherwise get.

The Hidden Vine

Website: http://www.thehiddenvine.com/wine-bar-san-francisco
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Moderate
Description: This is a relatively small place tucked in an alley. I came in to meet with someone. they had a lot of available sofa's and seats, very cozy and comfortable place. They even had a little area for bocce ball.
The Black Horse London Pub

Website: http://www.blackhorselondon.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Inexpensive
Description: This is one of those places that's so unique and off the radar that you really feel like you've discovered something special by stumbling in here.

Broken Record

Website: www.BrokenRecordBar.com
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Inexpensive
Description: Went to Broken Record to check out the food. This place is a dive bar that also has a pretty decent menu. If you are just looking to get some food you walk all the way to the back. There is indoor and outdoor seating back there. You order and pay for food at a window.
