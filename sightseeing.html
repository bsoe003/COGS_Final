Sightseeing

Alcatraz Island

Website: http://www.nps.gov/alca/index.htm
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: $30/person
Description: Tour the world famous cells and feel what it was like to be an inmate of Alcatraz prison.

The Presidio

Website: http://www.nps.gov/prsf/index.htm
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Free
Description: Get an outdoor feel of the area with over 25 miles of hiking trails or relax and watch the water from the nearby rocks and pier.

Fort Point

Website: http://www.nps.gov/fopo/index.htm
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Free
Description: Step into the life of someone who oversaw the building of the Golden Gate Bridge from this historic point in San Francisco or get a glimpse of how sailors were able to navigate the treacherous land edges of the San Francisco bay using the lighthouses that have been at Fort Point.

Lombard Street

Website: http://www.sftodo.com/lomabardcrookedstreet.html
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Free
Description: Visit one of the steepest streets in San Francisco. Go during Spring and you'll be able to see the amazing sight of all of the different colors of flowers lining this street. This street contains some of the priciest real estate in all of San Francisco.
Golden Gate Park

Website: http://sfrecpark.org/parks-open-spaces/golden-gate-park-guide/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Free
Description: With all of the vast land of Golden Gate Park you will always be able to find things to do here. There are gardens, playgrounds, lakes, picnic groves, trails, and monuments, plus an array of cultural venues, events, and activities happening year round.

Fisherman's Wharf

Website: http://visitfishermanswharf.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Free
Description: Historic Fisherman's Wharf is the classic San Francisco experience where you will find a range of hotels, specialty restaurants, famous attractions, unique shops, breathtaking scenery and entertainment for the whole family.
