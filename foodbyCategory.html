Food of San Francisco

Chinese


U-leesf
Website: http://u-leesf.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: under $10.00
Description: U-Lee offers the finest Chinese food in the Nob Hill neighborhood of San Francisco, and you don’t even have to leave home to get it. Our fast and friendly delivery service and extremely convenient online ordering system mean that top-notch Asian comfort food is always just a few mouse clicks away. Also available dine-in or to-go, everything on our menu is prepared in-house daily using only the freshest ingredients and finest meats and seafood.

San Tung Chinese Restaurant
Website: http://www.santungrestaurant.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: $11.00 - $30.00
Description: San Tung Chinese Restaurant offers some of the finest Chinese food in the Inner Sunset District. They offer delivery and accept groupons. The Restaurant is great for groups and they accept credit cards. The place is great for kids and they provide curb side pick up of meals.

Hunan Homes Restaurant
Website: http://hunanhome.ypguides.net/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: $11.00 - $30.00
Description: Hunan Home's Restaurant has been a favorite among local Chinatown residents since it opened in 1983. The restaurant is owned and operated by the Yuan family, who is originally from Taiwan and has been in the restaurant business for decades. Hunan Home's Restaurant has won numerous awards for its exceptional food and service including the Golden Award for the "Best Bay Area Chinese Restaurant" in the Chinese World Journal. A must see in San Francisco!

Japanese


Kappa Japanese Restaurant
Website: http://www.kapparestaurant.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: above $61.00
Description: The omakase is expensive, but for a special occasion, it is worth the experience. Everything was beautifully displayed to compliment the unique and flavorful dishes. It was a delight to my taste buds bc it was a discovery of new flavors that were melt-in-your-mouth good!!! I didn't want the experience to end - that's how fun and amazing it was. The creativity behind these meals shows how much the chef enjoys what he is doing. The sweet woman who served us our tea and explained what all the dishes were reminded me of a Miyazaki character - I love this place!

Kiss Seafood
Website: www.KissSeaFood.com
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: above $61.00
Description: Exquisite! Absolutely the best sushi I have had in San Francisco. A quiet, small, gentle place.

Oyaji Restaurant
Website: www.Oyaji.com
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: $11.00 - $30.00
Description: This place can still hold their own even though they are located away from everything. The quality of the fish is great, you can taste authentic art of Japanese sushi making here. They make sure the fish is room temperature before serving.

Mexican


La Taqueria
Website: www.la-taqueria.com
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Under $10.00
Description: This place is delicious! The meat has really good flavor. And they pack their tacos and burritos with meat. I 've gone here twice: once I got a burrito and tacos. Both are excellent. They have the option of both soft and crunchy tacos. For the crunchy tacos, they partially fry a tortilla, fold it and put a soft tortilla on the outside. You have to try it. Also try their green hot sauce.

San Jalisco
Website: http://www.sanjalisco.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: $11.00 - $30.00
Description: The service is really, really sweet and attentive. The prices are unbelievably cheap, particularly for the amount of food you get. This isn't the sort of Mexican food that makes you feel good after your eat it. You won't be running a marathon. But you will be very satisfied and full and plotting your return trip (you know, in a month or so).

Pancho Villa Taqueria
Website: http://sfpanchovilla.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Under $10.00
Description: While fresh ingredients, no preservatives, and lean cooking are all important aspects of the Pancho Villa experience, we never forget flavor. After all, the flavor of the Mexican cuisine is what started it all, and it is still what sets us apart today. Or, as Leah Garchik over at the SF Chronicle puts it: "Pancho Villa, which to me is the French Laundry of taquerias."

Firefly

Vegetarian


Millennium
Website: http://www.millenniumrestaurant.com/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: $31.00 - $60.00
Description: Millennium Restaurant is dedicated to supporting the essential earthly concepts of organic food production, small farms, sustainable agriculture, recycling and composting. We cook with fresh produce delivered every day, and choose organic whenever possible. Indian


Little Delhi
Website: http://littledelhi.net/
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: $11.00 - $31.00
Description: Little Delhi is one of San Francisco's favourite destinations for tasty, authentic Indian food.
