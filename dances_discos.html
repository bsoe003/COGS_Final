Dances/Discos

Bootie SF

Website: http://www.toronado.com
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Moderate
Description: Large lounge with 6 bars (2 downstairs, 4 upstairs), 4 rooms (main floor; lounge, loft, and side room upstairs), and plenty of places to sit and rest your drinks and your feet. Doesn't get too crowded inside, but it does get fairly packed by 12am. Drinks are strong. Specials are pretty good and inexpensive. Don't have to wait long to get a drink with so many bars.
Mr Smith’s

Website: http://www.toronado.com
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Moderate
Description: Such a great place to be these days. There are not many places like Club Deluxe that are left out there these days. Tasty Pizza, great drink s and fantastic music all the time!

Bimbo’s 365 Club

Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Moderate
Website: http://www.toronado.com
Description: Classy place. There's someone in the bathroom ready with towels, hot water, and cologne. The bartenders all look like legitimate shakers and movers, unlike the normal hipsters at San Francisco cocktail joints. The interior is darkly lit, but not so dark that you can't see anyone. Jazz musicians play while you eat and wait, and the venue floor is also quite large.

Butter

Website: http://www.toronado.com
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Inexpensive
Description: If you're looking for great music, a fun casual vibe, and interesting people.. well, Butter is your place.
Mighty

Website: http://www.toronado.com
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Moderate
Description: This is a great place to have a good time where people act like grown ups, respect each other, and most of all DANCE.. I have felt nothing but good vibes at this spot, and they always have the dopest guest DJ's spinning house or hip hop, depending on the night . When DJ Shortkut is a resident DJ you know this is the place to be.

The Showdown

Website: http://www.toronado.com
Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Price: Inexpensive
Description: Great drinks if you are looking for basic. Price is super cheap. Bartenders are attentive and not in a hurry. The open area in the back is a good little place to start a dance floor.
