Food under $10.00

The Codmother Fish and Chips

Website: http://www.the-codmother.com
Rating: ★★★★☆
Description: The owner is insanely friendly. The fish tacos were quickly made and were very tasty. The fried fish was very light and very good. Definitely a go to if you'd like a quick stop for lunch while in Fisherman's Wharf.
Ike's Sandwiches

Website: http://www.ilikeikesplace.com
Rating: ★★★★☆
Description: Very good savory sandwiches. Grab a sandwich from Ike's, walk over to Dolores park, and enjoy the goodness. Good plan for a nice sunny afternoon in San Francisco.

HRD Coffee Shop

Website: http://missionchinesefood.com/
Rating: ★★★★☆
Description: Excellent Chinese Food. The tea smoked eel is delicious. For spicy food lovers. Service is fast and attentive. Even during the week there is a line to get a table

Pan Grill Sandwich Shop

Website: http://www.thesaladplace.com
Rating: ★★★★☆
Description: By far one of the best sandwiches I had in San Francisco! I tried the Pork Katsu and the Smashed Tsukane Chicken Meatball, and they were to die for!!! I'm craving it as I write this review haha. Pan Grill will easily be on my next stop when I visit SF!
The Salad Place

Website: http://www.thesaladplace.com
Rating: ★★★★☆
Description: Bomb sandwiches and salads, as well as great items in the deli case, at a reasonable price. SF is inexplicably missing enough of these kinds of fresh and simple, but amazing, sandwich places.

El Farolito

Website: http://www.elfarolitoinc.com
Rating: ★★★★☆
Description: Crazy huge burritos and not expensive. Super busy when we stopped by for dinner. A bumping place with a fun atmosphere! I got the super pork burrito with no beans and it WAY too much food. Whatever that green sauce stuff is is COUNT. Try it out!
